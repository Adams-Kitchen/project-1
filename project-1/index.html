About Me

I started dancing when I was about 10 years old.
All of my friends were doing it, so I thought I
would give it a try. Now, 14 years later, it brought
me to BYU and none of my old friends dance anymore haha.
I danced for many different studios and teachers in high school, but
once I got to BYU, I started dancing with the BYU Ballroom Dance Company.
I danced on a team for 4 years, and I loved every second of it.
It was a lot of work, but it was so much fun.
It took me all over America and the world, and I made so many friends on 3 different continents.
